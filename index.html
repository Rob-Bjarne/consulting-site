We partner with founders, revenue leaders, and investors to solve complex growth challenges. Whether you're launching a new market, overhauling your GTM strategy, fixing team performance, or at an inflection point, Skald Advisory brings calm, clarity, and commercial impact.

How can we help?

Every engagement is different — here are a few ways we often work with clients.

Go-to-Market Strategy

We design scalable, data-driven GTM models aligned to your ICP, channels, and growth stage. From inbound/outbound sales to PLG and partnerships, we help you unlock sustainable revenue.

Sales & Revenue Advisory

We improve sales performance, structure teams, and implement operating cadences to drive pipeline, predictability, and accountability.

Pricing & Product Packaging

We refine pricing, messaging, and packaging to increase ACV, reduce sales friction, and better align with customer value.

Interim GTM Leadership

We provide short-term executive cover and special ops support for CRO or GTM roles, helping you drive transformation, hiring, and clarity during inflection points.

International Expansion Strategy

We support entry into UK, US, or APAC markets — with tailored go-to-market, team design, messaging, and localisation guidance.

Mentoring & Executive Coaching

We offer founder, executive, and emerging leader coaching. This is especially valuable in fast-growing startups where leadership responsibilities often outpace support. Think of it as exec-level mentorship with a pragmatic lens.

About Me

Rob Long is the founder of Skald Advisory. As Workable's first sales hire and eventual CRO, he helped grow the company from 10 people to over 300, reaching $50M+ ARR across global markets. Rob has also led Sales, Product Strategy, Product Marketing, Partnerships, and HR. His strength lies in solving messy growth problems with structured thinking and hands-on action. He brings breadth, calm, and credibility to every room — whether advising founders, mentoring functional leaders, or steering GTM strategy.
